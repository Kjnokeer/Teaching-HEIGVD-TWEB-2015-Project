MaMaThiVa

MaMaThiVa is a project which permit to a speaker to create a set of questions and let the audience answer on their device (PC, phone, tablet) just by transmitting a short URL.

Features

Easy polls

Create and share polls easily through an intuitive web interface!

Easily respond to polls that others have created!

Stats & Charts

You can watch the results of your polls in graphs!

As well as display a number of different and useful statistics about your polls!

Community

Being part of a growing community!

Share your best polls to other members of this site!
